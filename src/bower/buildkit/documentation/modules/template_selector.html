Landscape Book Starter Template

With a standard landscape size of 10 x 8 inches (25 x 20 cm) and a variety of cover and paper types, you can choose the ideal combination to fit your creative preference and budget.

  • 1
  • 2
  • 3

This template contains all the layouts required to create a graphically rich landscape book—choose from variety of layouts that contain images and copy. This style is basic greyscale, allowing you to define, and apply, your own color palette.

Formats Supported:
Hardcover Dust Jacket, Hardcover ImageWrap, Softcover & ebook
Template Pages:
24 unique pages, plus front & back covers
Fonts Used:
Helvetica, Garamond
Palette:
Grayscale
Suggested Uses:
Photography, Fine Art, Travel, Wedding & Family, Crafts & Hobbies
Version Supported:
Blurb BookWright 1.0 or greater
Software:
Blurb BookWright

Download Template

  1. Ready to use this template? Click the Download Template button
  2. Locate the .blurb file, in your Download folder or Desktop.
  3. Double-click the .blurb file to launch BookWright.
  4. Chose Save As and give your book an original filename.
  5. Review the starter templates tips to get started.
