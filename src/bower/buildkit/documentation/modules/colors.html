Primary Colors

The following is a list of the primary colors used in the Blurb brand. The accent colors are a variation of cold electric colors. These are only meant to be used on special occasions, when emphasis cannot be given through the usual monochromatic palette. Black, gray, and white is where we should exists 90% of the time.
