Triplets for Marketing

A group of three responsive columns inspired by stocksy.com. They expand and contract to the full-width of the viewport, stacking for mobile. They can appear on their own without copy, or with any embedded content (a headline and copy with no button, or just a button with no copy). The copy and buttons can be black or white (use button, typography, or other visual classes available). Also, content is always centered both vertically and horizontally, and the full area of each column is clickable.

Asset dimensions should be 500×300.

Limit paragraph content to 120 characters or less.
