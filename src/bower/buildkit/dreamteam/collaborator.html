I started publishing books with my small press, Publishing Genius, in 2007. Now I've translated the experience I've gained working on 40 books into helping other small presses and self-publishing authors get their work into the world. I will manage everything from editing books, to the design and print and eBook process, to building simple websites and simple publicity campaigns. These books have been reviewed in the NY Times, Kirkus, Publishers Weekly, and more. Authors get their books sold in independent bookstores as well as Barnes & Noble and (of course) Amazon.
